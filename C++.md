### 空着的就反斜杠加该字符
---
|指定字面值类型|方法|
|------------|----|
|前缀|写在前面的|
|u|char16_t|
|U|char32_t|
|L|wchar_t|
|u8|char|
|后缀|写在后面的|
|u or U|unsigned|
|l or L|long|
|ll or LL|longlong|
|f or F|float|
|l or L|long double|
